Our Story

A Home Away From Home

Founded in 2010, The Cozy Cub began as a small dream to create a welcoming space where people could gather, relax, and enjoy quality food and drinks. What started as a humble coffee shop has grown into a beloved community hub, known for our artisanal coffees, homemade pastries, and warm atmosphere.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting nearby farmers and producers. Our baristas are trained in the art of coffee making, ensuring every cup is perfect.
